About Me

Hi there!

My name is Nafiul Islam and I am what you'd call a software engineer. I'm deeply passionate about technology, and I spend most of my waking hours either writing code or reading about how to write better code. In my spare time I stay up to date with the latest tech news (Yes, just a little obsessed). If you like what I write, and think I'd make a good addition to your engineering or evangelism team, don't hesitate to contact me at nafiul[at]sopython[dot]com or gamesbrainiac[at]gmail[dot]com.

This blog is a collection of some of the things that I've encountered in my learning endeavors. If there's anything that I've said that you disagree with, or perhaps can offer a better solution than what I've proposed please let me know in the comments (that's what its there for) since I try my best to learn from my mistakes and the suggestions of others.

I answer questions on StackOverflow, and hang out in the Python chatroom regularly because I genuinely believe that giving back to the community is an integral part of being a software engineer.

I right now work in Amsterdam, writing a lot of Python code. If you're an awesome python programmer, and would like to work in Amsterdam, send me your CV.

Thank you for taking the time to know more about me :)
